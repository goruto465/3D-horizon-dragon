# Product Category Templates

Use these categories to organize your 3D toy inventory.

## 1. Articulated Critters
* Flexible, print-in-place designs.
* *Examples:* Dragons, Sharks, Octopuses, Geckos.
* *USP:* No assembly required, highly tactile.

## 2. Modular Building Blocks
* Custom pieces that snap together.
* *Examples:* Space station kits, castle walls, marble runs.
* *USP:* Infinite expandability.

## 3. Educational Gadgets
* Functional toys that teach physics or engineering.
* *Examples:* Centrifugal launchers, gear boxes, sundials.

## 4. Personalized Figures
* Toys where the user can add a name or choose a head shape.
* *Examples:* "Mini-Me" heroes, trophy toppers.
