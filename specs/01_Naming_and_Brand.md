# Brand Identity & Naming Strategy: 3D Toy Shop

This document outlines the core identity and naming options for your upcoming 3D toy shop.

## 1. Top Naming Contenders

### The "Tech-Forward" Category
Focuses on the 3D printing process and geometric precision.
* **VoxelPlay:** Modern, digital, and catchy.
* **The Z-Axis Toybox:** Intelligent and niche; appeals to makers.
* **PolyGo!:** Implies speed, low-poly aesthetics, and action.
* **Mesh & Maker:** Professional and craft-oriented.

### The "Whimsical" Category
Focuses on the magic of creation and play.
* **Dimension Den:** Alliterative and cozy.
* **DreamForge Toys:** High-end feel; suggests "forging" dreams into reality.
* **WonderPrint:** Simple, friendly, and very clear on the service.
* **Imagine3D:** Direct and inspiring.

### Brand Voice
* **Innovative:** We are at the edge of manufacturing technology.
* **Accessible:** 3D printing sounds complex, but we make it easy for kids and parents.
* **Creative:** Every toy is a canvas for the imagination.
